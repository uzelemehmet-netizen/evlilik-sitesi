Cancellation & Refund Policy

This page is a general information notice. Exact cancellation/change/refund terms are provided in the written official offer (and annexes) and supplier rules (airline/hotel etc.).

1) General Principles

  • Refundability depends on booking stage (ticketing/confirmation), dates and supplier conditions.
  • Some components (airline tickets, hotels) can be non-refundable or refundable with deductions.
  • After certain dates, refunds may be partial or not possible.

2) Changes

Date/name/itinerary changes are subject to availability and supplier policies. Price differences may apply.

3) Refund Processing

If a refund is applicable, the amount and method follow the Turkish agreement, the official offer/annexes and payment provider/bank processing timelines.

Refunds are generally processed via bank transfer (EFT/FAST/SWIFT) unless a mandatory legal/payment-provider rule requires otherwise.

4) Minimum Participants

If the minimum participant threshold cannot be reached, the Organizer may cancel the tour in accordance with applicable laws and the Turkish agreement.

5) Force Majeure

In case of force majeure (natural disasters, official decisions, pandemics etc.), the solution method is evaluated according to applicable laws and the official offer/annexes.

6) Priority of Turkish Documents

In case of any conflict, the Turkish documents prevail: Paket Tur Sözleşmesi (TR) and the written official offer and annexes.
